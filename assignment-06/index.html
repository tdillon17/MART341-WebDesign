Peaches & Cream Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • cream cheese
  • shortbread cookies
  • butter
  • gelatin packets
  • peaches
  • heavy whipping cream
  • vanilla extract
  • lemon juice
  • hot water
  • cold water

Directions

  1. Grease and line a 9 inch round spring form pan.
  2. Add shortbread cookies to a food processor. Pluse until finely crushed and add melted butter.
  3. Add cookie crust mixture to pan and freeze for 10 minutes.
  4. Add 2-3 quarted peaches to food processor and blend until smooth
  5. In small bowl combine 1/2 cup cold water and 2 packets of gelatin. Let bloom for 5 minutes. Microwave for 20-30seconds or until gelatin is melted.
  6. In large bowl beat the cream chesse, 1 1/4 cup sugar, vanilla extract, and lemon juice together with electric mixer.
  7. Add heavy whipping cream and melted gelatin and beat for 2-3 minutes until fluffy.
  8. Spread half the cheesecake mixture over the chilled cookie crust and freeze for 10 minutes.
  9. Mix 1 cup of peach puree to remain cheesecake mix. Carefully pour over the first layer of cheesecake. Spread evenly and freeze for 10 minutes.
  10. Combine gelatine mixture, hot water, and 2/3 cup sugar and whisk to desolve sugar.Let sit to cool.
  11. Starting from the outside working inward arrange sliced peaches in a circular patter.
    12. Spoon the gelatin over the peaches. Carefully put it in the refrigerator to set for 30 minutes. Pour the remaining gelatin mix over the peaches to the rim of the pan.
  12. Chill in refrigerator overnight before releasing from spring form pan.
