Here's why

My instructable is about how to do push ups. I chose this project because it was the only thing that I could think of and it was simple. Push ups mean a weird amount to me since I am enlisted and the last six years of my life has had a large portion of it dedicated to doing push ups. I do not recommend joining the service to learn how to do push ups, but you can watch work out videos, or go to a gym and learn from a personal trainer.
